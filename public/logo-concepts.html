BrewTech CC — Logo Concepts 2026

Modernising Africa's leading brewery instrumentation brand · Founded 1996 · John Dryden

Concept 01 — Precision Mark

Bold geometric mark built from the "B" form. Clean stacked wordmark — BREW large, TECH CC spaced in gold. Modern, technical, unambiguous. Works at any size.

Concept 02 — Vessel Icon

Fermentation vessel mark — nodding to brewing heritage while the clean sans-serif wordmark and tagline push it firmly into 2026. Tells the story without saying a word.

Concept 03 — Diamond Precision

Diamond monogram mark with two-tone wordmark (BREW white / TECH gold). "Africa · Est 1996" honours the founding without being dated. Premium, sharp, confident.

Concept 04 — Pure Wordmark

Ultra-clean stacked wordmark. Gold left bar anchors the brand. BREW bold/white, TECH light/gold — the weight contrast creates instant hierarchy. CC badge is subtle but present. Scales to any medium.
